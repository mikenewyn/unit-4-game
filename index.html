Crystal Collector

How to play:

The goal is to meet the "Target Number" by clicking on the crystals.

Each crystal is randomly given a value at the beginning of the game, but you don't know what it is until you click it.

If you hit the target number, you WIN! But we careful, if you go over, you LOSE!

The game will reset once you win or lose, and will keep track of your wins and losses.
